TAJ MAHAL

Discover the mesmerizing world of the Taj Mahal in the historic city of Agra, India. Agra's climate, featuring hot summers and cool winters, ensures a delightful experience year-round. The Taj Mahal, a testament to love and architectural brilliance, awaits your exploration in this culturally rich destination.

These are few attractions of the Taj Mahal:

Architectural Grandeur:

The Taj Mahal is celebrated for its exquisite Mughal architecture. The main mausoleum is built entirely of white marble and features intricate carvings, delicate inlays of semi-precious stones, and impressive domes. The symmetry and precision in its design showcase the skilled craftsmanship of the artisans from the Mughal era. The architectural beauty of the Taj Mahal has earned it a place among the New Seven Wonders of the World.

Symbol of Eternal Love:

The Taj Mahal was commissioned by the Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan as a mausoleum for his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal, who died during childbirth. The monument is a testament to the enduring love Shah Jahan had for his wife. The story behind its construction adds a romantic and emotional dimension to the Taj Mahal, making it not only a marvel of architecture but also a symbol of eternal love.

Gardens and Reflecting Pool:

Surrounding the Taj Mahal is a meticulously planned garden with lush greenery and pathways. The garden is divided into quadrants, and in the center lies a large reflecting pool that perfectly mirrors the grandeur of the main structure. The reflection in the pool enhances the visual impact of the Taj Mahal and adds to its overall charm. The gardens and the reflecting pool contribute to the serene and picturesque ambiance of the entire complex.
